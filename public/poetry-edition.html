Weekly Lab Reflection – Poetry Edition

This week’s lab reflection will take the form of a short poem.

After completing the lab, reflect on your experience and use an AI tool to express that experience as a poem. The poem can be in any style you like (free verse, rhyming, humorous, dramatic, etc.).

Examples

Programming / Debugging

"The code ran once, then failed again,
A stubborn bug, a silent friend.
One missing brace, one careful glance,
And suddenly the program danced."

Web Development

"A blank white page upon the screen,
Divs misplaced where none had been.
A line of CSS restored the view,
And order rose from chaos anew."

Cybersecurity Lab

"The scanner searched the silent night,
Revealing ports in glowing light.
A system tested, flaws made known,
Defense now stands where risk had grown."
